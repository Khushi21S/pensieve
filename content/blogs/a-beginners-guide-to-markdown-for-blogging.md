Markdown makes formatting content simple.

### Basic Syntax

- **Bold:** `**text**`
- *Italic:* `*text*`
- Headings: `#`, `##`, `###`
- Lists: `- item` or `1. item`
- Code: \`inline\` or fenced blocks

Use it to keep your blog content clean and portable.
